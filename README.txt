DEBUGGING CONTROLS
HOME - reset character to center of screen
LEFT MOUSE BUTTON - print to console x,y of mouse

1 - Decrease health by 1
2 - Increase health by 1
3 - Decrease gold by 10
4 - Increase gold by 10
5 - Spawn random amounts of health and gold pickups
6 - Spawn an enemy in a random location

f1 - Enter Waypoint mode
	Right - Change which enemy is selected
	Left - Save current patrol route to disk
	Up - Load next saved patrol route from disk
	Down - Clear current patrol
